# Business Model & Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

The AI Enhancement Microservice targets mid-market companies (200-2,000 employees) with a hybrid SaaS + consumption-based pricing model, emphasizing partnership-led growth through ATS marketplace integrations.

## Target Customer Profile

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Company Size**: 200-2,000 employees  
**Hiring Volume**: 50-500 hires/year  
**Pain Points**:
- Inefficient manual resume screening
- Lack of diversity in candidate pipeline
- Risk of AI discrimination (if using basic tools)
- Limited HR technology budget
- Existing ATS investment to protect

**Decision Makers**:
- **Primary**: VP/Director of Talent Acquisition
- **Economic Buyer**: CHRO / VP HR
- **Technical Influencer**: HR Systems Administrator
- **Legal/Compliance**: General Counsel (for Bias Module)

**Budget**: $15K - $75K annually for HR tech

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Partnership-Led Growth (Months 1-6)

**Primary Channel**: ATS Marketplace Integrations

**Strategy**:
1. **Greenhouse Partnership Launch** (Month 1-3)
   - Complete Greenhouse Marketplace certification
   - List on Greenhouse App Marketplace
   - Co-marketing launch webinar: "AI-Powered Hiring for Greenhouse Users"
   - Target: 5 pilot customers

2. **Lever Partnership** (Month 3-4)
   - Lever Partner Directory listing
   - Joint case study with pilot customer
   - Target: 3 additional customers

3. **Workable Integration** (Month 4-6)
   - Self-service integration documentation
   - Webinar series targeting Workable users
   - Target: 7 customers

**KPIs**:
- 3 active marketplace listings by Month 6
- 15 customers acquired through partnerships
- 50% of sales from partner referrals

### Phase 2: Direct Sales & Content Marketing (Months 6-12)

**Sales Motion**: Inside sales with product-led growth

**Team**:
- 2 Account Executives (AEs)
- 1 Sales Development Rep (SDR)
- 1 Customer Success Manager (CSM)

**Sales Process**:
1. **Inbound Lead** (from content, marketplace, demo requests)
2. **SDR Qualification** (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
3. **AE Demo** (45-min product demo + ROI calculator)
4. **Free Trial** (14-day trial with sample data)
5. **Procurement** (Legal/Security review, MSA signature)
6. **Onboarding** (CSM-led, 30 days)

**Average Sales Cycle**: 45-60 days (mid-market typical)

**Content Marketing Engine**:
- **Blog**: 2 posts/week
  - "How to Eliminate Bias in Job Descriptions"
  - "EEOC Compliance for AI Hiring: A Complete Guide"
  - "5 Signs Your ATS Needs an AI Upgrade"
  
- **Webinars**: Monthly
  - "AI in Hiring: Practical Applications"
  - "Passing the NYC Bias Audit"
  
- **Case Studies**: 1 per quarter
  - "How [Customer] Reduced Time-to-Hire by 40%"
  
- **Whitepapers**: 
  - "The State of AI in Recruitment 2025"
  - "Bias-Free Hiring: A Technical Guide"

### Phase 3: Enterprise Expansion (Year 2)

**Strategy**: Move upmarket to enterprise accounts

**Enterprise Sales Motion**:
- Hire VP of Sales
- Add 3 Enterprise AEs
- Partner with system integrators (Deloitte, Accenture)
- Attend HR Tech conferences (HR Tech Conference, Unleash)

**Target**: 20 enterprise customers ($100K+ deals)
